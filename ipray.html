Taking the Gospel to the Streets

Revival begins when believers take the Gospel beyond the walls of the church. We stir, train, and mobilise the Body of Christ to be a visible, vocal, and loving witness.

What We Stand For

Our Six Pillars

Pillar 01

Street Preaching

Boldly proclaiming the Gospel in public spaces — town centres, high streets, and community gathering points. We go where the people are.

Pillar 02

Worship in Public Spaces

Taking praise beyond the four walls. Live worship in parks, streets, and public venues — creating an atmosphere for God to move.

Pillar 03

Prayer & Intercession

Standing in the gap for our communities. Praying with and for people on the streets — believing God for breakthrough, healing, and transformation.

Pillar 04

Healing Ministry

Witnessing God's power through healing. We pray for the sick and hurting — and God shows up. Physical, emotional, and spiritual restoration.

Pillar 05

One-on-One Evangelism

Personal conversations that change lives. Every encounter matters. We share the love of Jesus face-to-face, one person at a time.

Pillar 06

Equipping the Church

Training and mobilising believers to step out in faith. We equip the Body of Christ with the confidence and tools to join the mission on the streets.
